Projects

Evaluation of QAC methods

Query Auto Completion are the suggestions you get as you type in your query in the search box. We aim to measure the quality and performance of QAC systems in this project.

Content dynamics of image meme based communities

How similar are the dynamics of meme based communities to that of text based communities. We try to explain the community dynamics by categorising each day based on temporal variations in the user engagement.

Conversation dynamics in e-commerce space

We analyze data related to e-commerce promotional events from Twitter and see how the conversation related to the event gets propagated in the network. We find that there is a small set of users who drives the conversation volume and the conversation graph converge to a bow-tie like structure.

Remote healthcare system - Healthie

Attempts to develop a remote healthcare system to help the patients do a self helth-check up. [Not involved after the intial phase of this study]
